How to Get to Valeria di Notte Zoo

The zoo is located in the heart of Animalia City and is easy to reach by public transport, car, bike, or even on foot!

Address

Valeria di Notte Zoo
123 Safari Lane
Animalia City, 00000

Map

Getting Here

  • By Bus: Take line 15 or 27 and get off at "Safari Lane" station.
  • By Train: Arrive at Animalia Central Station and walk 15 minutes or take bus 27.
  • By Car: Follow the signs to Safari Lane; on-site parking available.
  • By Bike: Dedicated bike paths lead directly to the zoo entrance.
