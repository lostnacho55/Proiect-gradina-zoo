How to Get to Valeria di Notte Zoo

The zoo is located conveniently just 4km from Timișoara city center, with multiple transport options: public transport, car, bike, or even on foot!

Address

Valeria di Notte Zoo
Drumul european E671, KM 4, vis-a-vis de Ikea
Timisoara, Timis

Map

Getting Here

  • By Bus: Take line M45 or E1 and get off at "Ikea" station, the zoo will be just across the street!
  • By Train: Arrive at Gara de Nord Station and take bus line E1 or a taxi.
  • By Car: Follow the signs to Calea Aradului; on-site parking available.
  • By Bike: Dedicated bike paths lead directly to the zoo entrance.
