Our Mission

At WildZoo, we’re dedicated to education, conservation, and the awe-inspiring beauty of the animal kingdom. Our mission is to inspire love and respect for wildlife in every visitor.

Our Story

Founded in 1985, WildZoo began as a small community project and has grown into one of the region’s most loved attractions. With over 300 species and a passionate team, we bring the wild closer to you every day.
