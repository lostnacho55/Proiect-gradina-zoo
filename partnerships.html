Our Partnerships

At Valeria di Notte Zoo, we believe collaboration creates real change. Through our partnerships, we amplify conservation efforts, drive research, and build community.

Strategic Conservation Partners

Our closest allies in protecting species and ecosystems globally. These organizations help us implement long-term conservation strategies.

Academic & Research Collaborators

We partner with Romania’s brightest minds to advance wildlife medicine, habitat conservation, and ecological research. These alliances bridge cutting-edge science with hands-on zoo expertise.


Featured projects: Genomic studies of Carpathian lynx (UVT), sustainable zoo nutrition (USAMV), Danube Delta biodiversity mapping (ICBIO).

Community & NGO Alliances

Rooted in Timișoara, we work with local advocates to inspire environmental stewardship. Together, we’ve planted urban wildlife corridors, trained 500+ educators, and hosted free nature therapy sessions.


  • Ecoteca: Joint "Urban Wildlife Watch" citizen science program
  • Verde și Viitor: 10,000 native trees planted in Banat since 2020

Corporate Sponsors

Romanian industry leaders help us innovate sustainably. Their support funds solar-powered exhibits, subsidized school visits, and endangered species breeding programs.


"Supporting Valeria di Notte Zoo aligns with our commitment to Banat’s sustainable development. Their conservation work inspires our employees and customers alike."

— Ana Popescu, CSR Director, Banca Transilvania
