Opening Hours

Monday – Friday: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM
Saturday – Sunday: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Holidays: Closed on public holidays

Tickets & Pricing

  • Adults: €12
  • Children (under 12): €6
  • Seniors: €8
  • Groups: Contact us for discounts

Facilities

  • Cafés & picnic areas
  • Souvenir shop
  • Restrooms & baby changing stations
  • Wheelchair access available

How to Get Here

By Car: Parking available on site.
By Public Transport: Take Bus 22 to “Zoo Entrance” stop.
